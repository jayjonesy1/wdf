h1
h1 is used for the main heading of your webpage. The one that is most important, and shows it as bigger text than the rest.
<h1>Headline here</h1>
h2
h2 is used for the first subheading of your page. It is the 2nd most important and a bit smaller than h1.
<h2>Sub-headline here</h2>
h3
h3 is used for another subheading within the first. It would be the 3rd important bit of text and much smaller than h1 & h2.
<h3>Sub-subheadline here</h3>
p
p is used to write a plain text paragraph with spaces above and below the writing.
<p>Paragraph Text Here</p>
ul
ul is an unordered list and lists the items shown in bullet list form.
<ul>List Items Here</ul>
ol
ol is an ordered list and lists the items shown in a numbered list form.
<ol>List Items Here</ol>
li
li is used for a list item. You add it to the ul & ol tags in order to show each individual listed item.
<li>Item Name Here</li>
